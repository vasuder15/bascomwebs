Overview

The goal of the Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) program is to give students the information and abilities needed to think like hackers and defend against cyberattacks.

Certified Ethical Hacker Exam & Certification

  • +   Who provides the Certified Ethical Hacker course certificate and how long is it valid for?

    • Upon successful completion of the Certified Ethical Hacker course, Bascom Bridge will provide you with an industry-recognized CEH Certificate after training completion which has lifelong validity

  • +   What are the prerequisites for CEH certification by EC-Council?

    • A learner can enroll in an official EC-Council CEH training course. Upon completion, a learner can then take the certification exam.

  • +   How do I schedule/book my CEH exam on EC-Council portal?

    • A learner must purchase an EC-Council kit. The kit includes courseware (digital format) and an exam voucher. Once training is complete, the learner must create an account on the EC-Council exam portal, select the desired exam and schedule the test.
